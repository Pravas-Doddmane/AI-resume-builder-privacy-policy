How to Request Account Deletion

AI Resume allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data. If you would like to delete your account, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email to 53cpravas@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – AI Resume
  3. Mention the email address used to sign in with Google

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User authentication data (Firebase account)
  • Resume content created within the AI Resume app

⚠️ Once deleted, the account and associated data cannot be recovered.
