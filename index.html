Introduction

AI Resume is an Android application developed by an independent student developer. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the AI Resume application.

Information We Collect

The app collects information only when users voluntarily provide it, including:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • GitHub Profile Link
  • LinkedIn Profile Link
  • Portfolio Link
  • Education Details
  • Skills
  • Work Experience
  • Projects
  • Achievements
  • Courses / Certifications

Authentication

AI Resume uses Google Sign-In for authentication. When signing in, Google provides basic profile information such as email address and a unique user ID, which is used only to identify your account within the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Create, edit, and manage resumes
  • Generate resume content using AI
  • Allow downloading and sharing resumes as PDF files
  • Maintain app functionality and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored using Google Firebase services, including Firestore and Firebase Storage. All data transfers between the app and Firebase services are protected using secure HTTPS (TLS) encryption.

While industry-standard security measures are used, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Data Deletion

Users can delete resumes from within the app interface. Please note that deleted resumes may not be immediately removed from backend storage and may be retained for operational or maintenance purposes.

Users may also request complete account and data deletion by visiting our Delete Account page.

AI Content Generation

AI Resume uses the Gemini AI API to generate resume content based on the information entered by the user. Only resume-related text is sent to the AI service for the purpose of generating content.

Advertisements

The app displays advertisements using Google AdMob, including banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) to provide and improve advertising services.

Personal resume data and content are not shared with advertisers.
